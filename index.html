DESIGN PROJECTS

This site is a work in progress!

Last term, I collaborated with a team of students on a mockup overhaul of UVic's current club management system. The current system is a Google Spreadsheet housing all club information (e.g. mission statements, contact info, etc.), which may easily overwhelm new students with information overload.

Our redesign attempts to incorporate the external club management system into the existing UVic Student Society website. Beyond improving accessibility by removing the need to visit an external site/service, using an existing design language should increase familiarity with a new user-interface.

I was responsible for creating the UI for the new administrative tools in Figma, which you can view in the embedded slideshow below

In late 2022/early 2023, I created and hosted a portfolio site to apply for a story internship at Walt Disney Animation Studios. Beyond storyboards, the site also showcases comics, illustrations, and some short cartoons (as well as an as-of-now outdated version of my resume).
